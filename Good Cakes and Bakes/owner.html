The Author of This Stunning Bakery

Hi everyone! Welcome to Good Cakes & Bakes! My name is Sanjida Islam. Since I enjoy sweets so much and brownies are such a little delicacy that can make anyone's day, I had always imagined opening a pastry store. I promised myself when I was seventeen years old that when I grew up, I would create my own Korean-style Bakery decor and sell brownies, and here we are! I'm overjoyed that my dream came true. Seeing my clients' delighted expressions as they bite into my brownies is quite delightful. I adore it so much! I'm grateful to everyone for helping me realize my aspirations!
